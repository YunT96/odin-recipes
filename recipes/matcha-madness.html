Experience the Zen: Dive into our Matcha Madness Bubble Burst

Embark on a journey of tranquility with our Matcha Madness Bubble Burst, where the serene essence of matcha green tea meets the playful pop of boba pearls. Delight in the harmonious blend of earthy matcha flavor and chewy tapioca pearls, creating a refreshing and energizing boba drink experience like no other.

Ingredients

  • Matcha green tea powder
  • Milk or non-dairy milk (such as almond milk or oat milk)
  • Sweetener (such as sugar, honey, or agave syrup)
  • Tapioca pearls
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Brew Matcha Green Tea
    1. Heat water to about 175°F (80°C) (not boiling).
    2. In a bowl, add matcha green tea powder.
    3. Gradually add a small amount of hot water to the matcha powder and whisk until smooth and frothy.
    4. Add more hot water to fill the cup and whisk again.
  2. Prepare Sweetened Milk
    1. In a separate container, mix milk (or non-dairy milk) with sweetener of choice until well combined.
  3. Cook Tapioca Pearls
    1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
    2. Add tapioca pearls to the boiling water and cook according to package instructions until they become soft and chewy.
    3. Once cooked, drain the tapioca pearls and rinse them under cold water to prevent sticking. Set aside.
  4. Assemble the Drink
    1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
    2. Pour the brewed matcha green tea over the ice cubes.
    3. Add a spoonful of cooked tapioca pearls to the glass.
    4. Top with sweetened milk mixture.
  5. Serve and Enjoy
    1. Insert a wide straw into the glass, allowing drinkers to enjoy the boba pearls along with the matcha green tea and sweetened milk.
    2. Serve the Matcha Madness immediately and enjoy the refreshing and energizing flavors!
